About Me.

As a Software Engineer and Web Developer, my expertise lies in crafting beautiful and functional Web Applications, with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, complemented by experience in React and Angular. My enthusiasm for coding is fueled by the excitement of translating ideas into dynamic and user-friendly interfaces that captivate and convert. During my immersive seven-month internship at Carbon Six, I honed my skills, learning industry best practices such as Tailwind CSS and Umbraco that further enriched my proficiency.

During my 12-week boot camp at Skillscity, I undertook a transformative project in collaboration with IBM. This hands-on experience involved utilizing Whisper AI to develop an application aimed at training a model capable of translating thousands of Albanian audio files into English in real time. This project not only enhanced my technical proficiency but also showcased my ability to work on innovative solutions in collaboration with industry leaders.

  • My Skills Are: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, Typescript Git & GitHub, Bootstrap, Tailwind, Umbraco, Figma and Python.
